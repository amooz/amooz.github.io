At OpsLevel, I led the engineering org building the developer portal platform — a product that helps engineering teams understand, own, and improve their services.

The product includes a service catalog, automated scorecards, integrations with CI/CD and observability tooling, and an API for teams to build on top of.

Key engineering challenges: keeping catalog data accurate without requiring manual updates, designing a scorecard system that drives real behaviour change, and building integrations that work across the heterogeneous tool stacks of enterprise customers.
