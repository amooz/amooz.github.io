After years of running a minimal link-in-bio page at this domain, I rebuilt the site properly.

The old one was a Jekyll theme with a name, a tagline, and a handful of social links. It did the job — it pointed people somewhere — but it wasn't really a site. More of a placeholder.

This one is built on [Astro](https://astro.build) using the [Dasein](https://github.com/roicort/dasein) theme. It has a blog (you're reading it), a projects section, and a bit more about who I am and what I've worked on.

## What I'm planning to write about

Mostly things I've been thinking about in my work: engineering leadership, developer experience, platform engineering, the practice of building and running software teams. Occasional tangents.

I'm not going to promise a posting schedule. I'll write when I have something worth saying.
